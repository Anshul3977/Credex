# Unit Economics

## What a Converted Lead is Worth to Credex

Credex sells discounted AI credits. Average deal assumption:
- A startup spending $1,000/month on AI tools
- Credex offers 20–30% discount on credits
- Customer saves $200–$300/month, buys through Credex
- Credex margin: ~15% of credit value
- Monthly credit purchase: $1,000 → Credex revenue: ~$150/month
- Annual revenue per customer: ~$1,800
- Conservative LTV (18-month retention): **$2,700 per customer**

## CAC by Channel

| Channel | Estimated CAC | Basis |
|---------|--------------|-------|
| HN Show HN post | ~$0 | Organic, time cost only |
| Reddit posts | ~$0 | Organic |
| LinkedIn cold DM | ~$5–15 | Time cost ~10 min/lead |
| Paid Twitter/X ads | ~$80–150 | Estimated $2 CPC, 5% audit completion |
| SEO (6-month lag) | ~$20 | Once ranking, near zero marginal |

At $0 CAC for organic channels and $2,700 LTV: 
**payback period is immediate on organic.**

## Conversion Funnel

| Stage | Rate | Basis |
|-------|------|-------|
| Landing page → audit started | 35% | Typical free tool conversion |
| Audit started → completed | 65% | Short form, low friction |
| Completed → email submitted | 20% | Value shown first |
| Email → consultation booked | 8% | High-savings leads only |
| Consultation → credit purchase | 30% | Warm, pre-qualified lead |

**Blended: 1,000 visitors → 350 audits → 70 emails → 6 consultations → 2 customers**

Revenue per 1,000 visitors: 2 × $2,700 = **$5,400**

## Path to $1M ARR in 18 Months

Need: $1,000,000 / $1,800 avg annual revenue = **556 active customers**

Month 1–3: 50 customers (HN launch, Reddit, LinkedIn outreach)
Month 4–9: 200 customers (SEO starts working, referral loop)
Month 10–18: 556 customers (paid acquisition turns on with proven CAC)

**What must be true:**
- Audit completion rate stays above 40%
- Email capture rate above 15%
- Credex closes at least 30% of consultations
