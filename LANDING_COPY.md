## FAQ

**Q: Is this actually free?**
A: Yes. No credit card, no trial period. SpendLens is free because 
it's built by Credex — we make money when we help you buy discounted 
AI credits, not by charging for the audit.

**Q: What tools does it cover?**
A: Cursor, GitHub Copilot, Claude, ChatGPT, Anthropic API, OpenAI API, 
Gemini, and Windsurf. We add tools based on demand.

**Q: How accurate are the recommendations?**
A: All pricing data is sourced directly from vendor pricing pages and 
updated weekly. The audit logic is rule-based and conservative — 
we only flag genuine overspend, not manufactured savings.

**Q: Do I need to connect my accounts?**
A: No. You manually enter your tools and spend. No OAuth, no bank 
connections, no sensitive data required.

**Q: What happens after I submit my email?**
A: You get a confirmation with your audit summary. If your savings 
potential is over $500/month, a Credex advisor may reach out about 
discounted AI credits. We don't spam.
